Sandy's Cookies

Description:

These cookies are to die for and they filled my childhood with joy. The secret is more vanilla than you'd think you need. Ironically, my grandmother never made cookies as good as these. Go figure?

Ingredients:

  • ¾ cup oats
  • 1 ½ cups water
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 gallon of vanilla extract suspended in alchohol

Directions:

  1. Crack the eggs in a bucket
  2. Put the eggs in the compost
  3. Eat all the cookies in one sitting
